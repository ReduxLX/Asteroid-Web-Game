Asteroid

Gameplay:
You control a spaceship which can shoot plasma bolts
Your mission is to utterly annihilate as many asteroids as possible
Destroying 20 asteroids will advance you to the next level
However the asteroids will travel faster in higher levels
You have 3 lives, colliding with an asteroid consumes one life
The game's over once you collide 3 times
To begin the game press the START button below the game display
If the game's performance worsens after a while consider switching to direct movement
Instructions to switch to direct movement can be found in the thrust code block within asteroid.ts
To avoid mouse aiming problems, scroll down to the very bottom then press START

Game Controls:
Press [Q] to activate ship thrust || Press [Left Click] to fire
